# Privacy Notice for `@gpu-cli/agentis`

`@gpu-cli/agentis` is designed for local-first use.

## What happens to your data

- All processing happens locally in your browser.
- No data is sent to any server by `@gpu-cli/agentis`.
- Transcripts never leave your machine.

## Telemetry and tracking

- No telemetry
- No analytics
- No tracking

Any telemetry capability is deferred to a future phase and would require
explicit opt-in.

## Demo data

Any demo fixtures provided with the project are synthetic and anonymized.
